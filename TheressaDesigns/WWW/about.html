ABOUT US

Theressa Designs is an owner operated business providing bridal and alteration services. The owner, Theressa Huang, has had over 30 years worth of experience in sewing and dressmaking. Since migrating to Perth, Australia, 8 years ago, Theressa has had an incredible journey exploring the culture of Australia whilst working in the bridal industry. Theressa has a dream of creating Perth's finest Australian bridal boutique providing the best quality services. Now, we are aiming to make that dream come true!

At Theressa Designs, we stock high quality 'Ready to wear' dresses and accessories at very competitive prices. We assure you that you wouldn't be disappointed with the many choices of modern and stunning dresses available to you. These includes but not limited to bridal gowns, bridesmaid gowns, flower girls, mother of the bride or groom, school ball dresses, evening dresses and special occasion wear. Some of them are available for hire (please ask in store). We also offer 'Custom made' dresses in order to fit your needs perfectly.

We also provide the finest alteration services. So, please contact us to enquire about any alteration services you might need. You are welcome to leave a feedback with us by clicking on the Contact Us link. We are happy to learn about your experience and how we can improve our services for you!

We understand that you want to look your best during the wedding ceremony. Now, you can leave all your wedding worries with us! Please come in store for free consultation and you can enjoy our hospitality in a relaxed environment.
