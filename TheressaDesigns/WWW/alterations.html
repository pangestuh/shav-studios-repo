Alterations

At Theressa Designs, we provide the full range of alteration services. No job is too big and no task is too difficult. Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any queries about our alteration services.

We provide the following alteration services:
  • Shorten
  • Enlarge Size
  • Reduce Size
  • Laceup Back
  • Capsleeves
  • Bustle
  • Netting/underlining
  • Beading
  • Change Design/Style
  • Hire Hoop
  • Wedding Dress Dry Cleaning

We can also give a quote for the work required. Please be advised that we cannot give an accurate quote unless you can bring your gown to the store.
